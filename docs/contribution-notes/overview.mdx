# Contribution Notes

This section is the knowledge base for contributors to the BTP Crossplane provider. It collects architectural decisions, implementation patterns, and essential background knowledge that goes beyond what the code itself communicates.

Whether you are onboarding as a new contributor or revisiting a non-obvious design choice — this is the place to look first.

## 👐 Lets discuss
If you have a question always feel free to reach out on our official crossplane slack channel:
🚀 [#provider-sap-btp](https://crossplane.slack.com/archives/C07UZ3UJY7Q) 

💬 We'd love to discuss your ideas and contributions in person in our monthly community call as well. 💬
👉 Find more details [here](https://github.com/SAP/crossplane-provider-btp/discussions/515). 👈

💡 Also, join our offline discussions on new feature concepts in our [Github Discussions](https://github.com/SAP/crossplane-provider-btp/discussions/categories/ideas).


## What you'll find here

- **Architecture Decision Records (ADRs):** Documented reasoning behind significant design and implementation decisions, so the "why" is never lost.
- **Best practices:** Patterns and conventions established by the team for consistency and maintainability across the provider.
- **Developer guides:** Focused how-tos for specific technical areas — covering setup, testing, and provider-internal mechanisms.

## Guides

- [External Name Handling](/docs/crossplane-provider-btp/docs/contribution-notes/external-name-handling) — How external names are structured, the generation script, and how to annotate new resource types correctly.
- [Service Binding Rotation](/docs/crossplane-provider-btp/docs/contribution-notes/service-binding-rotation) — The rotation mechanism for service bindings and relevant implementation details.
- [Upgrade Tests](/docs/crossplane-provider-btp/docs/contribution-notes/upgrade-tests) — How to run and interpret the upgrade test suite.
